What Orvesca is

Orvesca is a Windows desktop tool that lets you arrange the windows open in your taskbar into an ordered workflow, move through them with keyboard shortcuts you choose, jump to a specific window with a custom key, attach notes to windows, and save and restore your workflow. Orvesca reads the presence of your open windows to do this; it does not read or transmit the contents of those windows.
